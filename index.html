SCRAMBLED EGGS

Eggs are one of my favourite book.Here is a recipe for deliciously rish scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbls butter
  • 2 tbls cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a fry pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted amd add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edges of the pan to the centerevery 20 seconds
